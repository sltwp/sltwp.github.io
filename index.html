About Us

We are a Mythic guild with the core group being made of friends that have been playing together for some time now. Our aim is to create an environment that people enjoy playing in. We are looking to have fun in game, but at the same time want to take the game seriously while progressing in new raids and other challenges.

With Antorus, the Burning Throne being our first Mythic tier as a guild, getting Cutting Edge was a great achievement for us and we want to continue to push for Cutting Edge in all the tiers in the next expansion too.

You can find classes and specs we are currently looking for below, as well as link to the application form. However, everyone is welcome to join the guild, even if you prefer not to raid or are playing a class we are not primarily looking for.

If you are interested in other games, we are sure you can find people to play with on our Discord.
