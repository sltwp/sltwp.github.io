    About Us



    The beginnings

    We are Alliance guild on Silvermoon EU founded on 7th July 2017. We've originally started as a Heroic only guild on Magtheridon and cleared HC To as our first raid tier.

    After that we transfered to Silvermoon to give Mythic raiding a shot. We managed to get together a great team and achieved Cutting Edge in Antorus, The Burning Throne.

    Battle for Azeroth so far...

    We had a rough start in BfA where we lost a lot of our core raiders from Legion because of burn out. Therefore we've decided it's best to take a break from Uldir and come back even stronger and more motivated for the next raid.

    Unfortunately, same faith met us in Battle of Dazar'alor and we took a break at 6/9 M. We haven't raided in The Eternal Palace at all but we merged together with our friends from Win Condition - Tarren Mill, who have joined us for Ny'alotha which helped us to make a very strong core team.

    Ny'alotha and onwards

    We're now starting our progression through Ny'alotha. Motivated and with team better than ever before. We raid 2 days per week, Wednesdays and Sundays from 20:00 until 23:00 server time (CET).

    Our goal is to provide friendly but focused environment with a singe goal in mind - achieving Cutting Edge. We're currently most in need of Ranged DPS, you can check specs in the section below or our wowprogress page where the information is most up-to-date.
