    About Us



    The beginnings

    We are Alliance guild on Silvermoon EU founded on 7th July 2017. We've originally started as Heroic only guild on Magtheridon and cleared HC ToS.

    After that we transfered to Silvermoon to give Mythic raiding a shot. We managed to get together a great team and achieved Cutting Edge in Antorus, The Burning Throne.

    Battle for Azeroth

    We're now progressing through BoD at a fairly consistent rate with a very decent team. We are raiding 2 days a week, Wednesdays and Sundays from 20:00 until 23:00 server time (CET).
    Our goal is to achieve Cutting Edge in all the following raids BfA has to offer. We're currently most in need of Ranged DPS, you can check specs in the section below or our wowprogress page where the information is most up-to-date.
