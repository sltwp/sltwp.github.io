    About Us



    The beginnings

    We are Alliance guild on Silvermoon EU founded on 7th July 2017. We've originally started as Heroic only guild on Magtheridon and cleared HC ToS. After that we transfered to Silvermoon to give Mythic raiding a shot. We managed to get together a great team and achieved Cutting Edge in Antorus, The Burning Throne.

    Uldir situation

    We had a rough start in BfA where we lost a lot of our core raiders from Legion because of burn out. Therefore we've decided it's best to take a break from Uldir and come back even stronger and more motivated for the next raid. We have some of our old players returning to the game and we want to continue building what we started a year and half ago.

    Going forward

    With that being said, we're looking for like-minded players to join us on this adventure. We have some players from guild Underrated-Aggramar joining us. We are raiding 2 days a week, Wednesdays and Sundays from 20:00 until 23:00 server time (CET) and our plan is to achieve Cutting Edge in all the following raids BfA has to offer in a more relaxed way.
